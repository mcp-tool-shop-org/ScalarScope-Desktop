# Privacy Policy

**ScalarScope** | Version 2.0.0 | Effective: January 2025

## Summary

ScalarScope is a **local-only** desktop application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Collection

**We collect nothing.**

- No analytics or telemetry
- No crash reports sent to servers
- No user accounts or registration
- No network requests to our servers

## Data Storage

All data stays on your device:

- Comparison history stored in local application data
- User preferences stored in local settings
- Exported bundles saved to locations you choose
- No cloud sync or remote backup

## Third-Party Services

ScalarScope does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Network Usage

ScalarScope may access the network only for:

- Loading files from URLs you explicitly provide
- Checking for updates (optional, user-initiated)

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect data, there's nothing to:

- Access or export
- Delete or correct
- Opt out of

## Contact

Questions about this policy:

- GitHub Issues: https://github.com/mcp-tool-shop-org/scalarscope-desktop/issues
- Repository: https://github.com/mcp-tool-shop-org/scalarscope-desktop

## Changes

This policy may be updated with new app versions. Changes will be documented in the [CHANGELOG](CHANGELOG.md).
